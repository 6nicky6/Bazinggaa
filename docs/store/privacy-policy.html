Bazingga⚡ Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026 · Applies to the Bazingga mobile app

Bazingga is built on a simple promise: Fast. Private. Expressive. We collect only what the app needs to work, we never show ads, and we never sell your data.

1. What we collect

  • Account info: your email address (and phone number if you sign in by SMS), display name, username, optional status and profile photo.
  • Your content: messages, photos, voice notes, and Moments you create. Moments expire automatically after 24 hours.
  • App settings: preferences like notifications, wallpapers, and circles, stored on your device and in your account.

We do not collect your contacts list, precise location, or advertising identifiers.

2. How your data is used

  • To deliver messages, calls, and Moments to the people you choose.
  • To power optional AI features (see section 4).
  • To keep the service safe (block and report tools).

3. Where your data lives

Bazingga runs on Supabase (database, authentication, and file storage). All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Access to your data is protected by server-enforced row-level security rules: only chat members can read a chat, and only the audience you pick can see a Moment.

4. AI features

Smart Replies, chat summaries, translation, mood detection, and BazinggaBot are powered by the Google Gemini API. When you use these features, the relevant message text is sent to Google for processing and is subject to Google's privacy policy. AI features are optional — you can ignore or disable them, and nothing is sent when you don't use them.

5. Sharing

We never sell your personal data. We share data only with the service providers above (Supabase, Google Gemini) strictly to run the app, or if required by law.

6. Retention & deletion

  • Messages you delete "for everyone" are removed for all participants.
  • Moments are deleted automatically after 24 hours.
  • You can request full account deletion at any time by contacting us (below); we remove your profile and content within 30 days.

7. Children

Bazingga is family-friendly but is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and note the new effective date above.

9. Contact

Questions or deletion requests: bazingga.app@gmail.com
